Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Accra. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most anticipated conferences in Africa, bringing together innovators, developers, and industry leaders.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 aims to empower the next generation of African tech leaders by connecting minds and ideas that can drive innovation and change.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Ama Ofori – A pioneer in AI education across West Africa, she inspired thousands of students to explore machine learning.

  • David Mensah – Lead Cloud Architect at a Fortune 500 company, David shared insights on building scalable infrastructure.

  • Yaa Boateng – Known for revolutionizing mobile banking in Ghana, Yaa’s keynote on inclusive finance was a game-changer.
